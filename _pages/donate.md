By donating, you can help us keep our infrastructure up and running, which includes this website, our mailing lists, and code repositories.
{: .lead}

## Donate via PayPal

Donations are kindly managed by the [Software Freedom Conservancy (SFC)](https://sfconservancy.org/).
Right now, the most convenient way to donate is via PayPal.
To make a donation, press the following button:

<a href="https://sfconservancy.org/donate/" type="button" class="btn btn-primary donate-button">
  <i class="fa fa-paypal"></i>
  Donate via PayPal
</a>

## Other Donation Options

Alternatively, you can visit the [donation page of the SFC](https://sfconservancy.org/donate/) for other donation options such as wire/bank transfer and paper checks.

Please do not hesitate to contact [us](mailto:board@lists.squeakfoundation.org) or the [SFC](mailto:donate@sfconservancy.org) should you have any questions.
